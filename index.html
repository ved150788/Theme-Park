~ Frozenland ~

Welcome to Frozenland where you will be whisked away to a place of enchantment and magic!

Enlist with the delightful Anna to help save her sister. Join the handsome Hans. Get up to mischief with Olaf as he hops around town with his naughtiness. Hitch a ride with the loyal reindeer, Sven.

Be careful not to get in the way of Elsa's spells or be forever frozen in the icy kingdom!

~ Restaurants ~

Dine at one of many restaurants in Frozenland. There are eateries that cater for tastes from all over the world.

Stop by the iconic Frozen Ices parlour and choose from over 100 ice cream flavours!

~ Shops ~

Gifts, toys, sweets - make sure to stop by Frozenland's shops to buy things to remember your fabulous journey through the magical kingdom!

There's something for everyone in the family.

~ Events ~

Join a parade every third night, where your favourite characters throw a party with fireworks and colours!

See the special plays never seen before in one of our secret exhibition spots hidden in the frozen kingdom.
